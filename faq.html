FAQs

Q: What is 'Case Alert'?
A: 'Case Alert' is a free, open-source service that provides lawyers with email alerts for their upcoming cases.
Q: How do I sign up for 'Case Alert'?
A: To sign up for 'Case Alert', simply visit our website and fill out the registration form with case details for tracking.
Q: How does 'Case Alert' work?
A: Once you've signed up for 'Case Alert', our system will automatically send you email alerts for your upcoming cases. These alerts will include important information such as the court information, judge information, case information, etc along with the entire cause list.
Q: Is 'Case Alert' really free?
A: Yes! 'Case Alert' is a completely free service for lawyers.
Q: How can I update my case alert details?
A: To update your case alert details unsubscribe to that alert. You can do this by clicking the unsubscribe button in the email that comes for this case alert.
Q: I am not able to add case alert. What happened?
A: Case Alerts is restricted to only 100 alerts a day. Consider unsubscribing your old alerts or waiting for current number of alerts to reduce.
